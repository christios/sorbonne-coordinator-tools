# SCEN Academic Coordinator Handbook

A working reference for the **procedures, systems, and people** involved in
coordinating the Science & Engineering (SCEN) programmes at Sorbonne University
Abu Dhabi (SUAD) — the Foundation Year in Sciences (FYS) and the Bachelor
programmes in Mathematics and Physics (L1–L3).

It is written for two audiences:

- **New joiners**, who need to understand the systems, the jargon, and who does
  what, quickly.
- **Working coordinators**, who need a step-by-step reference for recurring
  tasks — grade reporting, catch-up exams, disciplinary committees,
  requisitions, timetables, and more.

!!! info "Scope"
    This handbook currently covers the **SCEN department** (FYS, BSc Mathematics,
    BSc Physics, and the new Master's programmes). It does not cover other
    departments. It focuses on the **academic coordinator** role — the work that
    sits between the Heads of Department, Sorbonne Paris, Admissions, HR, IT, and
    the students.

## A note on how the department fits together

The academic coordinator is a **hub**. On a typical task you may be coordinating
between:

- **Heads of Department / Programme** — Dr Valérie Le Guyon (Physics),
  Dr Omar ElDakkak (Mathematics), Dr Lama Tarsissi (Foundation Year in Sciences).
  They own academic decisions and sign grade records.
- **Sorbonne Paris** — the academic partner that validates Bachelor grades and
  issues the official PVs and transcripts for L1–L3.
- **Admissions** — publish transcripts and student records; the student-facing
  system of record.
- **HR** — contracts, CID clearance, requisitions, timesheets.
- **IT / Registrar** — Banner, Blackboard, Portal access, PV templates, and
  grade-platform tooling.
- **Serco** — facilities and timetabling.

The [Key contacts](getting-started/contacts.md) page lists who to approach for
what.
