# Onboarding checklist

What a new SCEN coordinator needs in place, and who provides it. Not everything
arrives on day one — chase the blockers, because most of the job depends on
system access.

## Identity & HR

- [ ] **Employment contract** — signed via Adobe Acrobat Sign (HR sends the
      envelope; prepared by HR Operations, e.g. Dimah Al Masri).
- [ ] **Sorbonne staff ID card** — collected from HR Operations once ready.
- [ ] **Emirates ID** — processed via HR.
- [ ] **ADERP / Oracle Fusion new-hire journey** — HR assigns a series of
      mandatory onboarding tasks in ADERP (personal info, emergency contacts,
      documents, code of conduct, contracts). Complete them promptly; they are
      time-bound.
- [ ] **Insurance / medical** — handled through HR.

!!! note
    Some HR/ERP records may show a transliterated or alternate spelling of your
    name (Fusion/ADERP is a government-integrated system). Keep an eye on which
    name each system uses.

## IT & accounts

- [ ] **Sorbonne email + Microsoft 365** — your primary account
      (`firstname.lastname@sorbonne.ae`).
- [ ] **Microsoft Teams** — you'll be added to the department staff teams:
      *Sciences and Engineering Department*, *BSc in Physics & BSc in Maths*, and
      *Foundation year in Sciences*. These are where teaching instructions,
      student pastoral care, and grade files are shared.
- [ ] **MFA registration** — required to use ServiceHub and other IT platforms.
- [ ] **Desk phone / office** — via General Services.
- [ ] **Outlook on phone** — for email on the go.

Log IT issues and requests through **ServiceHub** (the IT Helpdesk icon), or call
the **IT Helpdesk on 02 656 9123** / visit Office 2.157. See
[Systems & platforms](systems.md) for the platform links.

## System access (the important part)

Access to these is what unblocks the actual work. Request each via IT / Registrar
(often routed through your fellow coordinator or the HoD):

- [ ] **Banner** — course & CRN creation, grade entry for FYS. See
      [Courses & CRNs](../procedures/courses-crns.md).
- [ ] **Portal (Registrar Portal)** — student lists, assigning professors to
      courses (*Faculty Services → Monitoring Courses*).
- [ ] **Blackboard** — assessments, materials, exams, catch-up submissions.
- [ ] **SRS / Argos** — downloading FYS transcripts (access granted by IT, e.g.
      Junaid).
- [ ] **SIMAS** — creating Banner IDs for Visiting Professors / PROA.
- [ ] **Shared drives** — the OneDrive/SharePoint *Archives for SCEN* library and
      the department Teams files (grades, timetables, teaching loads,
      PV-Transcripts).
- [ ] **Download permission on transcripts** — a separate permission you may need
      to request explicitly.

!!! warning "Access is often the bottleneck"
    Granting access at SUAD can be slow and is spread across IT, Registrar, and
    Admissions. Request everything early, and keep a note of what is still
    pending. Some rights (e.g. Apogée access for Admissions to pull L1 student
    info from Paris) have to be requested from Paris, not from local IT.

## First-week orientation

- Read the [Systems & platforms](systems.md) and [Glossary](glossary.md) pages.
- Skim the [Annual cycle](../reference/annual-cycle.md) so you know what season
  you're joining in (grades? catch-up? budget? timetables?).
- Meet your fellow coordinator and the three programme heads (see
  [Key contacts](contacts.md)).
- Ask to be walked through **one live grade upload** and **one requisition** —
  they touch most of the systems and people you'll deal with.
