# Getting Started

If you have just joined as a SCEN academic coordinator, work through these four
pages in order:

1. **[Onboarding checklist](onboarding.md)** — the accounts, IDs, and system
   access you need in your first days, and who provides each.
2. **[Systems & platforms](systems.md)** — Banner, Blackboard, Portal, SIMAS,
   SRS/Argos, the VP Onboarding app, and the HR/ERP systems. What each is for and
   which ones you actually input into.
3. **[Glossary & acronyms](glossary.md)** — PV vs transcript, CM/TD/TP, FYS, PROA,
   MDC, refus de compensation, maquette, and the rest of the vocabulary.
4. **[Key contacts](contacts.md)** — who does what, in the department, at Paris,
   and across HR / IT / Admissions / Serco.

!!! tip "The fastest way to learn the role"
    Learn it **around the tasks you actually perform**, not by trying to
    understand every system end-to-end. Pick the next real task (a grade upload,
    a requisition, a catch-up list), open the relevant [Procedure](../procedures/index.md),
    and follow it. The systems make sense once you've used them for a concrete
    purpose.
